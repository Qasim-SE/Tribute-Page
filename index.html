Nikola Tesla

The man who invented the 20th century

Here's a timeline of Nikola Tesla's life:

  • 1856 - Born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire
  • 1884 - Moved to the US and worked with Thomas Edison
  • 1888 - Introduced AC motor and transformer
  • 1891 - Invented the Tesla coil
  • 1893 - Demonstrated wireless communication
  • 1943 - Died in New York City
